Journey

The journey of self-discovery is the most important journey we can take. It is about finding yourself, accepting yourself and expressing that divine power within; uncovering and tapping into the very best we can be. It is a lifelong journey and it all starts with a single step; onto the mat.

Approach

The Breathe Easy is my home-based yoga studio situated in the heart of Sandton. It is a safe-haven for you to let go of it all. It is a place where you can finally breathe easy knowing that your happiness, health and wellbeing is top priority. The Breathe Easy is also mobile. Can’t come to the studio? Let me bring The Breath Easy to you.

Focus

Yoga is a powerful tool that we can use to let go of the past, so we can focus on the present moment in a much more positive way. Yoga has the power to balance and to heal. It has the power to unite body, mind and spirit so that we may experience the present moment in perfect harmony, with complete clarity and contentment.

Embrace

One important thing to keep top of mind as we navigate through this complex world is that the happiness we are continuously searching for in this life is already within us. We just need to take the time to uncover and embrace it. So what are you waiting for? Embrace it all.
